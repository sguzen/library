A four-component AI content pipeline that takes raw business input (voice notes, product descriptions, PDFs) and automatically generates a week of platform-appropriate social posts, scheduled and ready to publish — without the business owner touching a keyboard again. Agencies charge $600–$1,000 to build this. The components cost under $50/month.

## The Four Components

### 1. Content Input Layer
A frictionless way for the client to submit raw material weekly. Options:
- Google Form where they submit ideas, stories, or product updates
- Shared folder for voice recordings
- Weekly prompt email they fill out in 5 minutes

The principle: zero social-media thinking required from the client. They describe what they want to say; the system handles the rest.

### 2. AI Writing Layer (Claude / ChatGPT)
A prompt template that transforms any input type into platform-appropriate posts. The template must handle:
- Different platform tones: LinkedIn (thought leadership) ≠ Instagram (casual) ≠ Facebook (community)
- Different input types: product announcements, customer reviews, personal stories, tips, opinions

**Building the template:** test it against 5 input types — product launch, customer review, personal story, quick tip, industry opinion. When it produces usable content on the first pass for all 5, it's ready to sell.

### 3. Scheduling Layer
**Buffer** (free plan: 3 social profiles) is the starting point. Understand: how to create a posting schedule, add a content queue, and connect client social accounts. One hour of learning is enough.

Alternatives: Metricool (more profiles on free plan), Hootsuite (enterprise).

### 4. Automation Layer (Zapier / Make)
Connects the three components:
`Client submits form → Claude generates content → Buffer queue fills automatically`

When the client submits Monday's raw material, their entire week of content is ready without further input.

## 48-Hour Learning Plan

**Saturday: Build the system yourself**
- Hour 1: Understand the four components conceptually
- Hour 2: Write and test the AI prompt template across 5 input types
- Hour 3: Learn Buffer (connect accounts, set schedule, add posts)
- Hour 4: Build the automation in Zapier/Make end to end using your own social accounts

The moment you see a test input flow through the full system and appear as a ready-to-post piece in Buffer — that's the moment you can sell this.

**Sunday: Learn the sales conversation**

Clients don't care about Zapier. They care about time. The conversation to have:

1. Ask how many hours per week they (or staff) spend on social media: typically 5–12 hours
2. Apply their hourly value: at $40/hour, that's $200–$480/week = $800–$1,900/month
3. Compare to your setup fee: $600–$1,000 one-time, plus $50/month in tools
4. The ROI is obvious in the first month — and the system saves that time every week forever
