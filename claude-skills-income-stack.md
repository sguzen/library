A three-layer income model that combines productized Claude Skills, AWS cloud certifications, and scalable workshops to reach $15,000/month — built from $0 in six months with $570 in total upfront investment.

## The Three Layers

### Layer 1: Claude Skills — Recurring Revenue (~$4,500/month)

Sell Claude Skills as managed services on monthly retainers. The model: charge a setup fee ($2,000–$3,500) that covers upfront build time, then a monthly fee ($400–$650) for maintenance and support.

**Three skill types that sell well:**

| Skill | Monthly Rate | Niche |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Content Atomizer | $500/client | Podcast hosts — turns one long piece into 15+ short-form pieces |
| Weekly Autopilot Report | $400/client | Marketing agencies — auto-generates client reports every Friday |
| Support Shield | $650/client | E-commerce brands — drafts 80% of support responses from knowledge base |

**What makes it work:** Build for a specific niche, not "businesses in general." The niche focus sharpens both the pitch and the actual skill design.

### Layer 2: Cloud + AI Consulting — Project Revenue (~$7,500/month)

Cloud certifications unlock a premium tier of client who sees you as a technology consultant, not just a tool user. The three certifications to get (total cost: $550):

1. **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner** ($100) — foundational; changes how clients perceive you
2. **AWS Certified AI Practitioner** ($100) — covers ML concepts and AI services on AWS
3. **AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer — Associate** ($150) — the game-changer; positions you to architect full AI strategies

**Consulting services these unlock:**

- **AI Employee Onboarding** ($3,000–$5,000/project): audit a business, configure Claude Skills + MCP + cloud integrations, train the team. 1–2 per month.
- **AI Strategy Sessions** ($500–$1,000/session): 1-hour paid consultation mapping workflows to AI automation. 3–5 per month.

Cloud + AI certified consultants command $100–$200/hour. The certification premium adds $15–$30/hour over uncertified rates.

### Layer 3: Skill Packs + Workshops — Scalable Revenue (~$3,000/month)

**Skill Packs** bundle related skills into niche-specific systems and sell as one-time purchases:
- "The Creator Stack" (Brand Clone + Content Atomizer + Social Scheduler) — $1,500
- "The Agency Operations Kit" (Client Onboarding + Weekly Reports + Proposal Machine) — $2,500
- "The E-commerce Support Pack" (Support Shield + FAQ Analyzer + Review Responder) — $2,000

**Workshops:** Monthly 90-minute "Build Your First Claude Skill" session, capped at 20 seats at $199. Net ~$1,000/session after fees. More importantly, 30% of workshop attendees convert to consulting clients — the workshop functions as a sales funnel.

## Six-Month Timeline ($0 → $15K)

| Month | Key Actions | Income |
|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Build first skill; start AWS Cloud Practitioner study | $0 |
| 2 | Pass Cloud Practitioner; sell first skill setup ($2,500 + $500/mo) | $3,000 |
| 3 | Pass AI Practitioner; 2 more setups; first strategy session | $6,400 |
| 4 | Pass ML Engineer; first AI Employee Onboarding project ($4K); 4 recurring clients | $8,300 |
| 5 | First Skill Pack sale; 2 consulting projects; 6 recurring clients | $12,400 |
| 6 | Workshop launch; 9 recurring clients; 2 consulting + 4 sessions | $15,000+ |

Total upfront investment: $20 (Claude Pro) + $350 (AWS exams) + $200 (misc) = ~$570.

## Why It Compounds

The three layers feed each other: recurring skill clients provide cashflow stability; certifications increase consulting rates; workshops generate consulting leads. Neither layer works as well alone.

The opportunity narrows over time — Claude Skills as a service category was nearly empty six months before this was written. Market saturation follows awareness.
